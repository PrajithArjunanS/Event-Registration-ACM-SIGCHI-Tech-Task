The Why, The What, The How
My Build Story

Why this approach?

To meet the ACM SIGCHI Tech task, I designed a simple and reliable registration form with validation and logging. Hosting on Apps Script minimizes infrastructure effort, simplify deployment, and logs submissions directly into Google Sheets for instant verification.

  • Same-origin calls via google.script.run
  • Durable, auditable log in Registration_Log
  • Easy to demo & record for submission

What I built

A single-page landing with neon accents and micro-interactions. The form enforces basic validation, shows a success notification, and the backend appends Name, Email, and Timestamp to the sheet.

  • Fields: Name + Email (regex verified)
  • Storage: Google Sheet: Registration_Log
Frontend Validation

Both fields required; email must match a regex pattern.

Apps Script Call

google.script.run.registerUser({name,email}) → same origin, secure, fast.

Logging & Ack

Row appended to the sheet; UI shows a success notification.
